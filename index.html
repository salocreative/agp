The 2026 AGP Season is open.

Following the success of last year's inaugural Agency Grand Prix, we're coming back bigger, better, bolder and faster.

Over four races, nine agency teams, will battle it out to see which team will raise the Grand Prix Laurels and be crowned The 2026 Agency Grand Prix Champions.

Two indoor technical races and two outdoor sprints, will show us who truly has the drive to lead the pack.

This is adrenaline-fuelled fun, team camaraderie, and inter-agency competition at its best.
